How we Work

We agree on work descriptions

About Us

We specialize in helping African parents in the diaspora to access highly qualified teachers located in Africa. We have unlocked the secret to connecting brilliant minds and visionary parents together. Our clients get to choose from a broad level of expert tutors to work with them at a very competitive salary range. Because our teachers are sourced from emerging markets, we can afford to provide you with highly trained teachers at less than half of what an average tutor charges in your host country. We rely on data-driven techniques to recruit, onboard and manage our teachers for our clients, giving the client the leisure to focus on their jobs while we focus on the excellent education of their children. We source, we onboard, we outsource and we manage!
